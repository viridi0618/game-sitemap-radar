Contracts are pacts between humans and devils — you give something up, you get supernatural powers in return. Only Human characters can sign contracts. Fiends and Hybrids can't. Sign three of the same devil type and you can become a Fiend yourself.

## How to Sign a Contract
1. Find a devil in Free Roam, Shrine, Floor Zero, or Raid
2. Meet its sacrifice requirement (body parts, combat trial, etc.)
3. Accept the deal
4. Contract appears in your abilities — equip and use

## Breaking a Contract
If you break a contract, **you die permanently**. All progress lost. The only way out is a **Devil Eraser** — buy it from the Black Market behind the painting in HQ.

## Contract Combos
You can hold multiple contracts simultaneously. Stack S-tier abilities — but watch your debuffs. Too many sacrifices can cripple you.

# 十一、/beginner-guide — 新手攻略

## Page Title
Devil Hunter Beginner Guide — First 30 Minutes Walkthrough

## Meta Description
Devil Hunter beginner guide: race selection, HQ layout, how to get your first weapon, best division, Black Market unlock, and essential tips for your first 30 minutes.

## Hero
New to Devil Hunter? Don't waste your first hour guessing. Here's exactly what to do.

## Step-by-Step

### Step 1: Pick Your Race
- **Human**: Can sign devil contracts, flexible build paths, access to Public Safety divisions. Pick this if you're new.
- **Fiend**: Cannot contract. Has a fiend skill tree instead. No divisions. Different playstyle — harder for beginners.
- ⚠️ You cannot change your race later. Choose carefully.

### Step 2: Navigate HQ
After spawn, you're in Headquarters. Find Kintoki — the NPC with a golden question mark in the center. He sends you to meet all 6 division captains. Floor 1 has captains 1-3. Floor 2 has 4-6 (use the elevator on the opposite side of the room from Kintoki). Completing this gives you starting money (humans only).

### Step 3: Get Your First Weapon
You have 4 starter choices: Kitchen Knife, Combat Knife, Katana, or Broad Axe. **Pick the Katana** — it's medium speed, medium damage, and will carry you through early missions.

### Step 4: Pick Your Division
Division 1, 2, or 5. Any of these have easy starting quests. Division 2 also houses a crafting table — convenient. Avoid Division 3 and 4 as a new player.

### Step 5: Unlock the Black Market
HQ has a painting with a hidden door behind it. Go through — inside is the Black Market. Important items rotate every 15 minutes. This is where you buy Devil Erasers, rare weapons, and clan rerolls.

### Step 6: Buy a Skateboard
Skateboards speed up your map travel. Buy one as soon as you can afford it.

### Early Game Checklist
- [ ] Join the Devil Hunter Roblox Group (needed for code redemption)
- [ ] Redeem all active codes for free Yen and rerolls
- [ ] Complete your division starting quest
- [ ] Reach First Class Hunter rank
- [ ] Find the Black Market and bookmark it
- [ ] Buy your first upgrade weapon (Yukimura or Yamada)
- [ ] Sign your first contract (Bat Devil recommended)

# 十三、/faq — 常见问题

## Page Title
Devil Hunter FAQ — Common Questions & Answers

## Meta Description
Devil Hunter frequently asked questions: how to become a fiend, get scars, join the group, reach Floor Zero, change race, and more. Quick answers.

## FAQ Items

### How do I become a Fiend?
Sign 3 contracts with the same devil type → find the injured Fiend NPC in HQ → pay 10,000 Yen → transform. You lose all contracts. Permanent decision.

### How do I get scars in Devil Hunter?
Scars appear when you take certain contract sacrifices. For example, Fox Devil requires your right eye and flesh — this leaves visible scars. It's cosmetic but shows other players your sacrifice level.

### What's the best beginner contract?
Bat Devil. Zero sacrifice, free AoE damage, and wings give you map mobility. Sign it immediately.

### How do I reach Floor Zero?
Take the elevator in HQ (opposite side from Kintoki). Floor Zero has high-level NPCs, Curse Devil, and Future Devil contracts.

### Can I change my race later?
No. Human ↔ Fiend is a one-way choice. If you want to switch, you must delete your character and start over.

### How to get Kuchigatana?
Craft it at the Division 2 crafting table. Requires Katana Fragments (farm from missions and raids).

### How do I join the Devil Hunter Roblox Group?
Go to the Roblox game page → scroll down to Developer section → click Join Community. Required for code redemption.

### What happens if I break a contract?
You die. Permanently. All character data wiped. The only safe way to end a contract is with a Devil Eraser from the Black Market.

### What is the Black Market?
A hidden shop behind a painting in HQ. Refreshes every 15 minutes. Sells Devil Erasers, rare weapons, clan and fiend rerolls.

### How do I get Devil Eraser?
Buy it from the Black Market. It's the only safe way to cancel a contract without dying.

### What's the difference between Human, Fiend, and Hybrid?
- **Human**: Can sign contracts with devils. Most flexible.
- **Fiend**: No contracts. Has a fiend skill tree and ascended weapons. Must transform from human.
- **Hybrid**: The third path. Unlocked via Soul Realm quests. Gets transformation abilities that outscale both humans and fiends. Permanent.

### How do I get hybrid?
Complete the Soul Realm quest chain. Level requirement varies by hybrid type. Once transformed, you lose human form and contracts.

- ✅ 独立 `<meta description>` ≤ 160 字符
- ✅ `og:title`, `og:description`, `og:image` 独立
- ✅ BreadcrumbList JSON-LD
- ✅ FAQ 页有 FAQ JSON-LD Schema
- ✅ canonical URL
- ✅ 全站英文，零中文
- ✅ Last Updated 时间戳在底部
- ✅ Footer: 版权 + SAB wiki 链接 + G&B wiki 链接 + 免责声明
- ✅ robots.txt (Disallow: /api/)
- ✅ sitemap.xml

## 技术
- Next.js 静态导出（farever-wiki-template，同 SAB/G&B）
- GA4 嵌入
- AdSense 广告位
- 面包屑：Home > Section > Page
