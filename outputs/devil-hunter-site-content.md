How to Sign a Contract:
1. Find a devil in Free Roam, a Shrine, Floor Zero, or a Raid encounter.
2. Meet its sacrifice requirement (body parts, combat trial, materials, etc.).
3. Accept the deal.
4. The contract appears in your ability menu — equip it and use it.

Breaking a Contract:
Breaking a contract kills your character permanently. All progress is wiped.
The only safe cancellation method is a Devil Eraser (Black Market purchase).

Contract Combos:
You can hold multiple contracts at once. Stack S-tier abilities — but watch
your debuffs. Too many sacrifices can cripple your stats.

|---|---|---|---|---|

Quick Recommendations:
- Easiest: Division 1, 2, or 5. Division 2 houses a crafting table — convenient.

Reports are conflicting — some players say you can switch via Black Market

Verify in-game.

Instead of joining Public Safety, you can join the Yakuza faction. This gives
you access to exclusive contracts (Snake Devil, Zombie Devil) and Katana Hybrid.
However, it is a permanent choice — you lose access to Public Safety HQ and
all Floor Zero NPCs. The Yakuza path is higher risk with exclusive rewards.

Title: Devil Hunter Beginner Guide — First 30 Minutes Walkthrough
Meta Description: Devil Hunter beginner guide: race selection, HQ layout,
how to get your first weapon, best division to pick, Black Market unlock,
and essential tips for your first 30 minutes.

Hero: New to Devil Hunter? Don't waste your first hour guessing. Here's
exactly what to do.

Step 1: Pick Your Race
- Human: Can sign devil contracts. Flexible build paths. Access to all
  Public Safety divisions. Recommended for new players.
- Fiend: Cannot sign contracts. Uses a fiend skill tree instead. No
  divisions available. Harder for beginners.
- Warning: You cannot change your race later. Choose carefully.

Step 2: Navigate HQ
After spawning, you are in Headquarters. Find Kintoki — the NPC with a
golden question mark floating above his head in the center of the room.
He sends you to meet all six division captains. Floor 1 holds captains 1-3.
Use the elevator opposite Kintoki to reach Floor 2 for captains 4-6.
Completing this quest gives you starting money (for Human characters only).

Step 3: Get Your First Weapon
You can choose from four starter weapons: Kitchen Knife, Combat Knife,
Katana, or Broad Axe. Pick the Katana — medium speed, medium damage, and
it will carry you through every early mission.

Step 4: Pick Your Division
Division 1, 2, or 5. All three have easy starting quests. Division 2 has
the added bonus of housing a crafting table. Avoid Division 3 (requires
bounty contracts) and Division 4 (requires PvP kills) as a new player.

Step 5: Unlock the Black Market
There is a painting on the wall in HQ with a hidden door behind it. Walk
through and you enter the Black Market. Items rotate every 15 minutes.
This is where you buy Devil Erasers, rare weapons, and reroll items.

Step 6: Buy a Skateboard
Skateboards dramatically speed up your map travel. Buy one as early as
you can afford it.

Early Game Checklist:
[ ] Join the Devil Hunter Roblox Group (required for code redemption)
[ ] Redeem all active codes for free Yen and rerolls
[ ] Complete your division starting quest
[ ] Reach First Class Hunter rank
[ ] Find and bookmark the Black Market
[ ] Buy your first weapon upgrade (Yukimura or Yamada)
[ ] Sign your first contract (Bat Devil recommended — free, safe, useful)

Title: Devil Hunter FAQ — Common Questions & Answers
Meta Description: Devil Hunter frequently asked questions: how to become a
fiend, get scars, join the group, reach Floor Zero, change race, and more.
Quick, clear answers.

1. How do I become a Fiend?
Sign 3 contracts with the same devil type. Go to the injured Fiend NPC in
HQ. Pay 10,000 Yen. Transform. You lose all contracts permanently.

2. How do I get scars in Devil Hunter?
Scars appear visually when you take certain contract sacrifices. For example,
the Fox Devil requires your right eye and flesh — this leaves visible
scarring. Purely cosmetic but signals your sacrifice level to other players.

3. What is the best beginner contract?
Bat Devil. Zero sacrifice cost, free AoE damage pulse, and wings give you
map mobility. Sign it immediately as your first contract.

4. How do I reach Floor Zero?
Take the elevator in HQ — it is on the opposite side of the room from
Kintoki. Floor Zero houses high-level NPCs and the Curse Devil and
Future Devil contracts.

5. Can I change my race later?
No. Human to Fiend conversion is one-way permanent. To switch races you
must delete your character and start over from scratch.

6. How do I get Kuchigatana?
Craft it at the Division 2 crafting table. Requires Katana Fragments
(farmed from missions and raids).

7. How do I join the Devil Hunter Roblox Group?
Go to the Roblox game page. Scroll down to the Developer section. Click
Join Community. Required for code redemption.

8. What happens if I break a contract?
You die permanently. All character data is wiped. The only safe way to
cancel a contract is with a Devil Eraser purchased from the Black Market.

9. What is the Black Market?
A hidden shop behind a painting on the wall in HQ. Stock rotates every
15 minutes. Sells Devil Erasers, rare weapons, clan rerolls, and
fiend rerolls.

10. How do I get a Devil Eraser?
Buy one from the Black Market. It is the only safe way to cancel a
contract without dying.

11. What is the difference between Human, Fiend, and Hybrid?
- Human: Can sign contracts with devils. Most flexible playstyle.
- Fiend: No contracts. Uses a fiend skill tree with ascended weapons.
  Must be unlocked by transforming from human.
- Hybrid: The third path unlocked via Soul Realm quests. Gains unique
  transformation abilities that outscale both humans and fiends.
  Permanent choice — cannot go back.
